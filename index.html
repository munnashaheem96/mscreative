SSCET APP (Bete Version)

The SSCET Outpass App is a mobile application designed for students and faculty members of Sri Shanmugha College of Engineering and Technology (SSCET) to manage and streamline the process of requesting and approving outpasses. The app allows students to submit outpass requests, specifying details such as reason, date, time, and category (day scholar or hosteller). Faculty members, including class advisors and heads of departments, can review and approve or deny these requests. The app also includes features such as real-time status tracking, notifications, and easy access to attendance details. Designed with a user-friendly interface, the SSCET Outpass App aims to enhance communication, simplify administrative workflows, and provide a seamless experience for both students and faculty in managing outpasses efficiently.
